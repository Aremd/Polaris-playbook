Leverage Loop — Detail
ETH Loan = non-liquidatable. LTF is not a danger threshold — it is a borrowing capacity ratio. The risk is leverage risk (ETH drops → pETH collateral loses market value), not forced liquidation.
1-click loop coming — the manual sequence (buy pETH → open ETH Loan → buy pETH → adjust) will be replaced by a single atomic transaction via LI.FI composer, Enso, or natively in Polaris. Parameters: target LTF + max number of rounds.
Optimal context
Floor price ↑ over 7d · LTF available < 60%
ETH stable (±3%/24h) · No immediate macro event
LTF mechanics
LTF = ETH Debt / (pETH Collateral × Floor price)
Applies only to ETH Loan — not to the CDP
Efficiency / round ≈ floor ratio × LTF%
Ex: 0.2987 × 80% ≈ 23.9% per round
Natural convergence after ~4 rounds

Metrics circuit

Read left to right = understand the cause. Read right to left = trace back to the source of a problem.

ETH spot→ Oracle→ LTV (CDP)→ TCR system→ IR controller→ Net Borrow Rate→ SP APY→ Net Yield
LTF (ETH Loan) follows its own logic: LTF = ETH Debt / (pETH Collateral × Floor price). It does not feed into the causal chain above — the ETH Loan is non-liquidatable. LTF only indicates remaining borrowing capacity.
MetricScopeWhat it tells youWhat it does not tell youReflex to develop
LTVCDPHealth of your pUSD borrowing position. Distance to liquidation.Does not indicate system health. Low LTV in a low TCR = false sense of security.Always cross-reference LTV + TCR. Never one without the other.
LTFETH LoanETH borrowing ratio based on floor price. Indicates remaining capacity.Does not reflect liquidation risk — ETH Loan is non-liquidatable.LTF = leverage gauge, not a danger signal. Monitor floor price as the health indicator.
TCRSystemOverall system health. Recovery Mode if < 150%.Does not indicate whether your individual position is at risk.TCR < 175% → stay alert even if your LTV is low.
Net Borrow RateCDPReal cost of debt after pETH yield deduction. The only CDP profitability metric that matters.Not sufficient alone — compare to SP APY to calculate net yield.NBR > SP APY → SP First becomes unprofitable. Act within 24h.
IR controllerSystemProtocol gross rate. Softening in progress by the team.Does not reflect real cost (see Net Borrow Rate).Track the 7d trend, not the instantaneous value.
pUSD pegSystemStablecoin health. >$1.01 = demand > supply. <$0.99 = stress.A brief depeg is not a crisis. Automatic repeg observed in 27 min.Depeg > 1h below $0.98 = investigate. Brief = wait.
Floor price pETHSystemMechanical protocol growth. Can only go up.Does not indicate an ETH Loan liquidation price (none exists).Floor ↑ over 7d → favourable conditions for Leverage Loop.

SP APYSystemSP yield. Rises when depositors panic-exit.A high APY during stress is an opportunity, not a danger.SP APY rising during ETH drop → stay in, do not exit.
POLAR inflationSystemDilution pressure. Decreasing = active buyback = strong protocol activity.Does not reflect short-term POLAR price.Inflation < 5% over 7d = accumulation signal.
